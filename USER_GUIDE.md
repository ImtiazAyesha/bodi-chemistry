## What is Bodi Kemistri?

Bodi Kemistri is an AI-powered wellness assessment platform that analyzes your body alignment and posture using your device's camera. The system captures 4 different views of your body and provides a comprehensive wellness score with personalized recommendations.

---

## Getting Started

### System Requirements
- **Device:** Computer or laptop with a webcam
- **Browser:** Chrome, Edge, or Firefox (latest version)
- **Internet:** Stable connection required
- **Space:** Clear area with good lighting (about 6 feet from camera)

### Before You Begin
1. **Find a well-lit space** - Natural lighting works best
2. **Clear the area** - Remove obstacles behind you
3. **Wear fitted clothing** - This helps the AI detect your body better
4. **Position your camera** - At chest/shoulder height, about 3-6 feet away

## üìè Understanding Feedback Messages

The system gives you **two levels of guidance**:

### **Strong Commands** (When you're far from target)
- **"MOVE LEFT"** - Take a big step to your left
- **"MOVE RIGHT"** - Take a big step to your right
- **"STEP BACK"** - Move backward (away from camera)
- **"COME CLOSER"** - Move forward (toward camera)

### **Gentle Commands** (When you're close to target)
- **"A BIT LEFT"** - Small adjustment to your left
- **"A BIT RIGHT"** - Small adjustment to your right
- **"A BIT BACK"** - Small step backward
- **"A BIT CLOSER"** - Small step forward

### **Perfect Alignment**
- **"PERFECT! HOLD STILL"** - Don't move! Countdown starting!
- **Green outline** - Ghost turns green
- **Countdown timer** - 3... 2... 1... CAPTURED!

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### **"No arrows are showing"**
- Make sure you're in the camera view
- Check if your body is detected (green landmarks visible)
- Try moving closer or farther from the camera

### **"Arrows keep changing"**
- You're on the edge of alignment
- Make smaller adjustments
- Wait for "A BIT" messages for fine-tuning

### **"Countdown won't start"**
- You're not perfectly aligned yet
- Follow the arrows until they disappear
- Look for "PERFECT! HOLD STILL" message

### **"Camera is black/not working"**
- Refresh the page
- Allow camera permissions in your browser
- Check if another app is using your camera

### **"Turn indicator won't go away" (Stages 5 & 6)**
- You're not turned enough to the right
- Turn a full 90¬∞ - your right shoulder should face the camera
- Don't just turn your head, turn your whole body

### **"Capture keeps failing"**
- Hold still during the entire 3-second countdown
- Don't move even slightly
- Make sure lighting is consistent
